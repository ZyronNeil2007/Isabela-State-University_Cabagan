# Welcome to the ISU Premium ID Generator Wiki

The **ISU Premium ID Generator** is a powerful, client-side single-page application (SPA) built to generate high-fidelity, print-ready student identification cards for Isabela State University (ISU) - Cabagan Campus.

This Wiki serves as the comprehensive guide for developers, contributors, and end-users on how to use, configure, and extend the ID generator.

## 🌟 What makes it special?

Unlike traditional ID generators that rely on backend servers to process images and PDFs, this tool runs **100% locally in your web browser**.

- **No Servers:** Everything from image cropping to OCR scanning happens on your local device.
- **Privacy First:** Student data, photos, and signatures never leave your browser.
- **Professional Output:** Generates perfectly sized, high-resolution A4 PDFs with accurate cut lines at the standard CR80 ID dimensions.
